JAGRAN LAKE CITY UNIVERSITY, BHOPAL is a private university established under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act 1956 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

With practice-based pedagogy at its core, JLU Bhopal is one of the most awarded universities in Central India. More than 2500 students from 8 countries and 27 states of India attend the university, which currently offers 50+ degree programs.

Currently, JLU Bhopal is the only ERASMUS+ participating university for Central India under the Tuning India Project, which is funded by the European Commission, and serves as the country chair for Association of Universities of Asia Pacific (AUAP).
